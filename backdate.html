Daniola President and CEO Sinmi Adeoye-Esene among 200 participants in exclusive Climate Innovation Networking Breakfast

CALGARY, AB - Daniola Corp. President and CEO Sinmi Adeoye-Esene participates in the exclusive Women in Climate Innovation networking breakfast hosted by Canada's Trade Commissioner Service on September 19.

The invite-only gathering aims to convene a diverse group of 200 women from various corners of the globe, all dedicated to advancing energy transition, emissions reduction, climate innovation, and ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) portfolios. The attendees represent a spectrum of expertise ranging from the development of groundbreaking technologies and policies to investment attraction and talent education.

The panel features prominent venture capital and private equity investors focused on climate solutions. These esteemed investors provided invaluable insights into their investment strategies and offered guidance on navigating the evolving landscape of climate innovation. This event aims to facilitate continued networking and collaboration among participants. With Daniola partnering with mining businesses globally, Sinmi's presence at this event helps spark discussions on innovative solutions to address climate challenges.
